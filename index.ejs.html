24/7 Crisis Support
988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential support for people in distress. Prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones. Available 24/7 nationwide.

Crisis Text Line

Free, 24/7 crisis support via text. Connect with a trained Crisis Counselor who will listen and help you move from a hot moment to a cool calm.
